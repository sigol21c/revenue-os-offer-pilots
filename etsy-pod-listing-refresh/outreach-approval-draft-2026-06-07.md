## Buyer / JTBD

Desired outcome in one sentence:
> “Send one listing link; get 3 title options, all 13 Etsy tags, a rewritten first description paragraph, 3 change reasons, and one publish-readiness caution.”

## Monetization hypothesis
A manual `$99 launch pilot` listing refresh is easier to understand and buy than another internal tool. A small, approved reaction test should reveal whether the buyer problem is real through replies, objections, or paid/deposit intent.

7-day learning targets after approval:
- Strong: 1 paid/deposit intent or explicit request for payment link.
- Medium: 3 qualified replies from Etsy/POD sellers with real listing context.
- Weak but useful: 5 explicit rejection/objection reasons.
- Kill/rework signal: no replies or only confused responses after an approved, targeted test.
